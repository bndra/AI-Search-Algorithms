alpha-beta search with pruning and interative deepening for an AI that plays a game called Isolation on an 8x8 grid, like a chessboard.
The rules of Isolation:
  -players begin in opposite corners
  -players take turns moving their piece to a new location
     -can move in 8 directions like a queen in chess
     -cannot move through/onto opponent or previously occupied spaces
     -must move each turn
  -when a player can no longer make a move on their turn they lose
